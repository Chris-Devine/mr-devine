Website Designer/Developer / Manchester, UK / 2013 - Current

Alstom

Embedded Systems & Web Designer/Developer / Manchester, UK / 2012 - 2013

During my time at university, the train and electronic company Alstom approached me and my team to ask us to develop an embedded system capable of monitoring the utility meters in different parts of the their factories. The embedded system would be used to help Alstom determine how to best lower their carbon footprint. Me and my team in turn developed PUMR (Portable Utility Meter Reader). PUMR met their requirements by monitoring the meters and relaying data to a central database. This data was then displayed in chats and graphs on web pages we implemented into their current website.

TheGrowBot

TheGrowBot.com website is my personal project that I am spending my spare time developing. The aim of the project is to make the process of indoor horticulture fully automated at a low cost of entry. This is to help schools and hobbyists have the ability become involved in growing their own food while also enjoying the scientific learning curve. The rest is still top secret! Hopefully TheGrowBot website and products will be ready for release in 2017!
